THE PROBATION OFFICER/PROBATIONER RELATIONSHIP
Because of the authoritarian image, individuals under supervision sometimes surround the Probation Officer with a “professional stigma” which creates a barrier hampering rapport, development of friendship and guidance in certain areas.  THE VOLUNTEER........IS NOT STIGMATIZED BY THE PROFESSIONAL IMAGE OR LOOKED UPON AS A FIGURE OF AUTHORITY.  The very nature of the Probation Officer’s position requires a certain amount of aloofness in order to retain necessary controls and socializing with probationers could create jealousies and obscure objectivity.  THE VOLUNTEER....... CAN BE A FRIEND AND THE NATURE OF HIS VOLUNTARY SERVICE IS COMPATIBLE WITH THE IMAGE OF CONCERN AND GENUINE INTEREST.
